# ArbitraSaaS User Guide

Welcome to ArbitraSaaS – your autonomous multi-tenant Solana Arbitrage engine. This guide helps you navigate the platform and maximize yield.

1. **Dashboard Home**
   - The primary dashboard gives you a macroscopic overview of your Fleet (connected wallets), PNL over 30 days, average execution latency, and your most recent arbitrage logs.
   
2. **Connecting a Wallet**
   - Head to the "Wallets" tab via the Sidebar.
   - Click "Connect New Wallet". Input your private key string carefully. It is encrypted client-side immediately before transit and permanently safely guarded in our KMS.
   
3. **Configuration & Parameters**
   - Click "Configure" on a connected wallet. 
   - You can choose from 'High Volatility', 'Safe Mode', 'Jito Protected', or write a custom configuration. 
   - Modifying a configuration takes immediate effect via the Worker Engine.
   
4. **Billing & Subscriptions**
   - Upgrade your tier from the "Billing" Tab.
   - Free Tier supports 1 Wallet. PRO supports up to 10 wallets simultaneously.
   
5. **Security**
   - Please ensure you enable 2FA on the Settings page to prevent unauthorized configuration tweaks or wallet removal.
